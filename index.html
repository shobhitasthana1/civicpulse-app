How It Works
A simple process that turns citizen reports into real Kanpur improvements.
📸
Step 01
Report Issue in Kanpur
Capture a photo, add location details, and submit the problem in seconds.
Tap anywhere in Kanpur on the map or type the locality — Kalyanpur, Rawatpur, Kakadeo. AI auto-categorizes your issue.
📡
Step 02
Track Resolution
Monitor your report status and get live updates until it's resolved.
Receive push notifications at every step — assigned, in-progress, resolved. Full timeline with department updates.
👥
Step 03
Community Participation
Citizens and volunteers collaborate to highlight and improve local issues.
Upvote critical issues, join ward groups, earn civic points and climb the Kanpur leaderboard.
⚡
Step 04
Emergency Support
Use the SOS feature to quickly notify emergency services when urgent help is needed.
Direct escalation to Kanpur Police, KDA, Fire, and KESCO. Average response time: 4 minutes.
