❓ FAQ

Common Questions

How do I report an issue?
+
Open the Report Issue section, submit details like location, category, and priority, and you will receive a unique tracking ID instantly.
Is CivicPulse free to use?
+
Yes. CivicPulse is a completely free platform for citizens to report civic issues. We work in partnership with local municipalities.
How can I track my complaint?
+
Use the Track Reports page from the navigation menu. You can search using your tracking ID, title, or location to see real-time progress.
Can I upload photos of the issue?
+
Absolutely. You can attach image evidence (JPG, PNG) when submitting a report to help authorities understand the severity of the issue faster.
